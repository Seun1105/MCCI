What is MCCI

A Digital Civic Engagement Platform for Today's Youth

The Memetic Civic Communication Initiative (MCCI) is a digital civic engagement platform focused on improving civic literacy and community participation among youth in the United States.

MCCI leverages memes, storytelling, and participatory digital media to transform how young people access, understand, and engage with civic information. By aligning civic education with modern digital communication formats, the initiative bridges the gap between traditional civic knowledge and the media environments where youth actively engage.

Our Method

How We Work

MCCI operates at the intersection of communication, education, and digital culture.

01

Memetic Communication

We use culturally relevant formats, memes and short form digital content to simplify complex civic ideas and make them widely shareable.

02

Digital Storytelling

We highlight real community experiences and translate civic issues into relatable narratives that connect with lived realities.

03

Civic Education

We provide accessible resources explaining civic processes, public issues, and opportunities for community participation.

04

Community Engagement

We collaborate with organizations to support youth participation in civic dialogue and local community initiatives.
